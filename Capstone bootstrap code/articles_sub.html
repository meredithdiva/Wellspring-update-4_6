Articles, books, blogs & other reads

We've curated a collection of useful resources just for you. These are articles, books, and other readings that have proven helpful for others who've walked the same path. Learn about substance use disorder as a disease, the impact on relationships - and how setting the course for your own parallel recovery can begin, continue, and make a positive difference.

On substance use disorder as a disease

There are numerous books and publications to help us understand substance use disorder as a disease - one with symptoms and outcomes like diabetes or cancer. Explore publications that unpack the importance of separating the person from the illness.

Books, articles, research, websites, and columns

Looking at substance use disorder through the lens of science and disease

  • Broken: My Story of Addiction and Redemption by William Cope Myers and Katherine Ketcham
  • Now what? An Insider's Guide to Addiction and Recovery by William Cope Myers
  • The Real Dope on Dealing with an Addict: How Addiction Saved My Life by Meridith Elliott Powell
  • Understanding Addiction: Know Science, No Stigma by Dr. Charles Smith and Dr. Jason Hunt
  • Evaluating the Brain Disease Model of Addiction by Nick Heather
  • Unbroken Brain: A Revolutionary New Way of Understanding Addiction by Maia Szalavitz
  • We Are the Luckiest: The Surprising Magic of a Sober Life by Laura McKowen
